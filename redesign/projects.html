Our projects

Research that reaches the ground.

From safe water in Tajikistan to green hydrogen and radiation-resistant crops — a look at the work ISTC funds across its member states.

Tajikistan💧
Water Security · Tajikistan

Safe water for Istiklol

Rebuilding resilient water infrastructure for a Tajik city — and a model for cross-border water management across the region.

Kazakhstan🌾
Biosafety · Kazakhstan

Radiation-resistant wild cereals

Studying how native grasses withstand radiation — protecting food systems and advancing environmental science.

Tajikistan⚗️
Energy · Tajikistan

Green hydrogen by photocatalysis

Turning sunlight and water into clean fuel — a step toward the region's low-carbon energy independence.

Armenia🌱
Bioenergy · Armenia

Power from agricultural waste

Converting crop residue into usable bioenergy — cutting waste and emissions on Armenian farms.

CBRN🛡️
CBRN · Regional

CBRN readiness for mass events

Preparing first responders and organisers to keep large public events safe across Central Asia.

Regional🔐
Cybersecurity · Regional

Securing critical infrastructure

A new direction: protecting energy and water systems from digital threats through joint research.
