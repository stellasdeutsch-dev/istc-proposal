The highlight

ISTC Special Day in Osaka.

On 27 August 2025, ISTC hosted its Special Day at the Expo — a ceremony bringing together partners, donors and Japanese officials to mark decades of scientific cooperation and look ahead to the next chapter.

Alongside it, the Governing Board convened its 80th session in Osaka — turning a world's fair into a working milestone for the organization.

The stage is gone. The projects continue.

Many of the ideas showcased in Osaka are now funded, on-going projects across ISTC's member states.
