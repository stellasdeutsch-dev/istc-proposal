Our work

Science that keeps people safe.

Six connected fields, one mission: using research and collaboration to make the region — and the world — safer, more secure and more sustainable.

Nonproliferation

Redirecting CBRN expertise toward peaceful ends and preventing the spread of weapons knowledge.

Biosafety & Biosecurity

Strengthening how laboratories and nations manage biological risk, from pathogens to policy.

CBRN Risk Mitigation

Training and field exercises that prepare the region for chemical, biological, radiological and nuclear threats.

Water Security

Cross-border research and webinars protecting Central Asia's most contested resource.

Energy Security

Supporting clean and resilient energy — from green hydrogen to small modular reactor studies.

Science Diplomacy

Using research collaboration as a bridge between governments that might not otherwise talk.

How we work

How a project comes to life.

1

Idea & proposal

Scientists from member states submit joint research proposals, often with international partners.

2

Review & funding

The Governing Board reviews proposals and approves funding from partner governments.

3

Research

Teams carry out the work — in laboratories, in the field, across borders.

4

Impact & sharing

Results feed into safer policy, real-world applications and the global science community.
